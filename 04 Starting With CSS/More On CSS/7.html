Google Fonts

Google Fonts is a library of free and open-source typefaces that are hosted by Google. It allows web designers and developers to easily incorporate a wide variety of fonts into their projects. Google Fonts currently offers over 1,300 fonts, and the collection is constantly growing.

The fonts in Google Fonts are licensed under the SIL Open Font License, which means that they can be used for free for both personal and commercial projects. This makes Google Fonts a great resource for designers and developers who are looking for high-quality fonts that are affordable and easy to use.

In addition to offering a wide variety of fonts, Google Fonts also provides a number of tools and resources to help designers and developers use the fonts effectively. These tools include a web font viewer, a web font generator, and a CSS font loader.

Google Fonts is a valuable resource for anyone who needs to use fonts in their web projects. It is easy to use, has a wide variety of fonts to choose from, and is free to use.
